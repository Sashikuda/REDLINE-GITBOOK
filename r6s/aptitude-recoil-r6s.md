Be sure Discord Overlay Settings are as follows (copy it exactly!):

## [](#ensure-youve-copied-the-discord-overlay-settings-above)Ensure you've copied the Discord Overlay Settings Above

Visit aptitude.pub and enter the account information provided to you in your email, Format: Username, Password | DO NOT CREATE YOUR OWN ACCOUNT

After logging in, hover over the "customer area" and press on the loader button, you will get a file.

After downloading, ensure the Game & Anticheat are completely closed. Run our Loader next and log in there, choose the specific product (in your case R6) and press load.

After the loader CMD pops up saying everything is good to go, start your game!

Once fully in-game, you'll see a message at the top of your screen saying "Insert to Open Menu" (Self explanitory)

Please keep in mind that sometimes Aptitude will ask you to create a security question or possibly add an email to your account, always remember and retain access to all of the information you provide.

We don't have the power to reset anything security-related on your Aptitude account!
