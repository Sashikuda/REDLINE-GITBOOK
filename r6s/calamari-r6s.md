# 💻Calamari \[R6S\]

*   **Download the Loader:** Download the Loader from [this link](https://user.mempatch.com/) and extract it into a separate folder.
    

1.  Register with a username & password, then input your key.
    
2.  Download the loader and follow onscreen instructions.
    
3.  Press the "Insert" key on your keyboard, then the Calamari menu will appear, to close it simply press Insert again.
    

This product requires [Visual C++ Runtimes](https://closetware.gitbook.io/instructions/troubleshooting/visual-c++-runtimes) to be downloaded.
