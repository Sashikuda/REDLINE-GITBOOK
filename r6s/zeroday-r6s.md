Accessing the Web Based Menu:

To manage and customize your options and features, go to [myloader.cc](https://myloader.cc/) and click on the “Menu” option.

Changes you make will be applied live in-game, allowing immediate customization to enhance your experience.



ESP not showing/nothing working:

Check Discord Overlay settings. Legacy must be enabled, Regular Discord Overlay to be disabled, and Overlay for R6S enabled.

If Status on Overlay (in Discord settings) states "Initializing", Restart & try again. Still not working? Disable and reenable R6S overlay.

Join a Discord Call & Talk once to enable the Discord overlay.

Product stopped working Mid-game:

Check to see if there was a product update. Our Product uninjects on updates, requiring the user to reinject. This is due to our kill-switch.

Alt Tabbing a lot? This can cause issues with the overlay, which will stop displaying the ESP on Fullscreen. Use Borderless/Windowed instead.

Refreshed Discord/closed it? Discord is required to remain 24/7, without refresh or anything. This is due to the product using Discord Overlay. Restart game and reinject if so.
