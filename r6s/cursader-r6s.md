# 🟢﹒Cursader [R6S]

*   **Download the Loader:** Download the Loader from [this link](https://uniqueloader.com/r6s/) and extract it into a separate folder.
    

1.  Launch the loader and input your key.
    
2.  After the loader has loaded, Click "Start Injection Process"
    
3.  A message saying "Please Open Rainbow Six Siege" will appear, launch the game and wait until you are fully in the game.
    
4.  Press the "Insert" key on your keyboard, then the Crusader menu will appear, to close it simply press Insert again.
    

This product requires [Visual C++ Runtimes](https://closetware.gitbook.io/instructions/troubleshooting/visual-c++-runtimes) to be downloaded.
