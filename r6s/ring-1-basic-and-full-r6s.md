# Ring-1 Basic & Full \[R6S\]

System Requirements

*   **Processor**: Intel CPU
    
*   **Operating System**: Windows 10 or Windows 11 (must be updated to the latest version)
    
*   **BIOS Settings**:
    
    *   **Intel Virtualization**: Must be enabled. [How do I do this?](https://closetware.gitbook.io/instructions/troubleshooting/virtualization-and-hyper-v)
        
    *   **Secure Boot**: Must be disabled. [How do I do this?](https://closetware.gitbook.io/instructions/troubleshooting/disable-secure-boot)
        
    
*   **Anti-virus**: All anti-viruses, including Windows Defender, must be [fully uninstalled](https://closetware.gitbook.io/instructions/troubleshooting/disable-anti-virus).
    

#### 

[](#initial-setup)

Initial Setup

1.  **Download the Loader:** Download the Loader from [this link](https://ring-1.io/loader-key) and extract it into a separate folder.
    

#### 

[](#using-the-loader)

Using the Loader

1.  **Open the Loader**: Sign in using your site credentials (Or use your key)
    
2.  **Select Cheats**: Choose the cheats you wish to use, in this case R6S Basic or Full
    
3.  **Spoofer Settings**: The spoofer is enabled by default. It is not advised to disable it, but you can do so in the bottom right corner if necessary.
    
4.  **Load Cheats**: Click on "Load selected cheats". A green checkmark will indicate success.
    
5.  **Restart**: Click the "Restart now" button. Confirm when prompted.
    
6.  **Reboot**: Your PC will restart. Once rebooted, you can open the game—running the Client again is not required.
    

Please note that the cheat will always inject after restart, if you want to uninject shutdown your pc fully.

**Activate Cheats: Use the** `**DEL**` **key to open the cheat menu in the game.**
