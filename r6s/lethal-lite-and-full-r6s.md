# 🔴﹒Lethal Lite & Full [R6S]

System Requirements

*   **BIOS Settings**:
    
    *   **Secure Boot**: Must be disabled. [How do I do this?](https://closetware.gitbook.io/instructions/troubleshooting/disable-secure-boot)
        
    
*   **Anti-virus**: All anti-viruses, including Windows Defender, must be [fully uninstalled](https://closetware.gitbook.io/instructions/troubleshooting/disable-anti-virus).
    

#### 

[](#initial-setup)

Initial Setup

Create a Lethal Cheats Account on our official website [**Here**](https://lethalcheats.net/register)

1.  Click on "Redeem a License" in the top right corner and redeem your Key.
    
2.  **Download the Loader:** Download the Loader from the client area and run it.
    

Note that all loader's generated on your account will be tied to your key and account credentials, if you try sharing the loader file to someone else, it will not work.

#### 

[](#using-the-loader)

Using the Loader

1.  **Open the Loader**: Sign in using your site credentials (Or use your key)
    
2.  **Select Cheats**: Choose the cheats you wish to use, in this case R6S Lite or Full
    
3.  **Spoofer Settings**: The spoofer can be enabled optionally. It is not advised to disable it, but you can do so in the bottom right corner if necessary.
    
4.  **Load Cheats**: Click on "Load selected cheats".
    
5.  **Reboot**: Your PC will restart. Once rebooted, you can open the game—running the Client again is not required. The cheat will remain injected as long as you haven't rebooted/turned off your computer
    

Please note that the cheat will always inject after restart, if you want to uninject shutdown your pc fully.

**Activate Cheats: Use the** `**INS**` **key to open the cheat menu in the game.**
