### [](#how-do-i-disable-core-isolation)How do I disable core Isolation?

Open Start Menu: Click on the Start button or press the Windows key on your keyboard.

Access Settings: Click on the gear-shaped icon to open the Settings application.

Navigate to Update & Security: In the Settings window, click on "Update & Security".

Open Windows Security: From the left-hand menu, select "Windows Security".

Access Device Security: Click on "Device security" in the list of Windows Security options.

Open Core Isolation Details: Under Core isolation, click on "Core isolation details".

Disable Memory Integrity: Toggle the switch to turn off "Memory integrity".

Restart Your Computer: For changes to take effect, restart your computer.
