### [](#how-to-check-your-windows-version)How to Check Your Windows Version

Follow these steps to find out which version of Windows you are running:

Press Win + R on your keyboard to open the Run dialog box.

Type winver in the text field.

Click OK or press Enter.

A window will appear displaying your Windows version and build number.

Why is this important?
Some of our products only target certain Windows Buids, most commonly Windows 11 24H2 and above might not have full compatibility compared to Windows 11 23H2 and below - Always read the product description!Last updated 9 months ago
