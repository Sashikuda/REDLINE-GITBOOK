### [](#how-to-sync-time-on-windows)How to Sync Time on Windows

To sync your Windows PC's clock with an Internet time server:

Open Settings: Click on the Start menu and select "Settings" (⚙️).

Navigate to Time & Language: In the Settings window, choose "Time & Language."

Sync Your Clock:

Go to the "Date & time" tab.

Enable "Set time automatically" if not already enabled.

Scroll down and click on "Sync now" under the "Synchronize your clock" section.

Your computer will now synchronize its clock with the default time server.
