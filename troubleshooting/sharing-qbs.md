How to Locate `rainbowsix.exe` in Rainbow Six Siege

**For Steam Version:**

1.  Open Steam and navigate to your **Library**.
    
2.  Right-click on **Tom Clancy's Rainbow Six Siege** and select **Properties**.
    
3.  Go to the **Installed Files** section and click on **Browse** to open the game directory.
    
4.  Look for the `rainbowsix.exe` file within the directory.
    

**For Ubisoft (Uplay) Version:**

1.  Open the Ubisoft Connect (Uplay) client and go to **Games**.
    
2.  Find **Tom Clancy's Rainbow Six Siege** in your game library.
    
3.  Click on the game tile to open its overview.
    
4.  Select **Properties** and then click on **Open Folder** next to the **Installation Location**.
    
5.  In the opened folder, locate the `rainbowsix.exe` file.
    

This guide will help you successfully find the `rainbowsix.exe` file in both the Steam and Ubisoft versions of Rainbow Six Siege.

Once you have the file, upload it to a file host [https://limewire.com/](https://limewire.com/), then send it to one of our support members!
