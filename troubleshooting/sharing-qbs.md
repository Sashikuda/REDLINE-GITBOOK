[](#how-to-locate-rainbowsix.exe-in-rainbow-six-siege)How to Locate rainbowsix.exe in Rainbow Six SiegeFor Steam Version:

Open Steam and navigate to your Library.

Right-click on Tom Clancy's Rainbow Six Siege and select Properties.

Go to the Installed Files section and click on Browse to open the game directory.

Look for the rainbowsix.exe file within the directory.

For Ubisoft (Uplay) Version:

Open the Ubisoft Connect (Uplay) client and go to Games.

Find Tom Clancy's Rainbow Six Siege in your game library.

Click on the game tile to open its overview.

Select Properties and then click on Open Folder next to the Installation Location.

In the opened folder, locate the rainbowsix.exe file.

This guide will help you successfully find the rainbowsix.exe file in both the Steam and Ubisoft versions of Rainbow Six Siege.

Once you have the file, upload it to a file host [https://limewire.com/](https://limewire.com/), then send it to one of our support members!
