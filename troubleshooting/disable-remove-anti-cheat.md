# Disable/Remove Anti-Cheat

### 

[](#valorant-riot-vanguard-esea-faceit)

Valorant (Riot Vanguard), ESEA, FaceIT:

#### 

[](#uninstall-riot-vanguard)

Uninstall Riot Vanguard

1.  Open the **Start** menu and go to **Settings**.
    
2.  Select **Apps** and then click on **Apps & features**.
    
3.  Scroll through the list to find **Riot Vanguard**.
    
4.  Click on it and select **Uninstall**.
    
5.  Follow the prompts to complete the uninstallation process.
    
6.  Restart your computer to ensure all components are removed.
    

#### 

[](#uninstall-esea-client)

Uninstall ESEA Client

1.  Click on the **Start** menu and open **Control Panel**.
    
2.  Select **Programs** and then choose **Programs and Features**.
    
3.  Locate **ESEA Client** in the list of installed programs.
    
4.  Click **Uninstall** and confirm any additional prompts.
    
5.  Wait for the process to finish and restart your computer if necessary.
    

#### 

[](#uninstall-faceit-anti-cheat)

Uninstall FaceIT Anti-Cheat

1.  Go to the **Start** menu and select **Settings**.
    
2.  Click on **Apps**, then navigate to **Apps & features**.
    
3.  Find **FACEIT Anti-Cheat** in the list.
    
4.  Click on it and choose **Uninstall**.
    
5.  Follow the on-screen instructions to remove it completely.
    
6.  Restart your computer to finalize the uninstallation.

#### 

[](#uninstall-punkbuster-anti-cheat)

Uninstall PunkBuster Anti-Cheat

1.  Open the **Start** menu and go to **Control Panel**.
    
2.  Click on **Programs** and select **Programs and Features**.
    
3.  Find **PunkBuster Services** in the list of installed programs.
    
4.  Highlight it and click **Uninstall/Change**.
    
5.  Follow the prompts to complete the uninstallation.
    
6.  Once the process is complete, restart your computer to ensure all components are removed.
