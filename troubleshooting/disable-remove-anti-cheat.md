### [](#valorant-riot-vanguard-esea-faceit)Valorant (Riot Vanguard), ESEA, FaceIT:

[](#uninstall-riot-vanguard)Uninstall Riot VanguardOpen the Start menu and go to Settings.

Select Apps and then click on Apps & features.

Scroll through the list to find Riot Vanguard.

Click on it and select Uninstall.

Follow the prompts to complete the uninstallation process.

Restart your computer to ensure all components are removed.

[](#uninstall-esea-client)Uninstall ESEA ClientClick on the Start menu and open Control Panel.

Select Programs and then choose Programs and Features.

Locate ESEA Client in the list of installed programs.

Click Uninstall and confirm any additional prompts.

Wait for the process to finish and restart your computer if necessary.

[](#uninstall-faceit-anti-cheat)Uninstall FaceIT Anti-CheatGo to the Start menu and select Settings.

Click on Apps, then navigate to Apps & features.

Find FACEIT Anti-Cheat in the list.

Click on it and choose Uninstall.

Follow the on-screen instructions to remove it completely.

Restart your computer to finalize the uninstallation.

[](#uninstall-punkbuster-anti-cheat)Uninstall PunkBuster Anti-CheatOpen the Start menu and go to Control Panel.

Click on Programs and select Programs and Features.

Find PunkBuster Services in the list of installed programs.

Highlight it and click Uninstall/Change.

Follow the prompts to complete the uninstallation.

Once the process is complete, restart your computer to ensure all components are removed.
